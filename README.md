Project Description:
PETFIT is a fun interactive way to keep mind of your calorie goals. Built by team DasSums for HackForAll 2026: ACM Pre-Chapter, 
this application converts calorie tracking into a rewarding habit by linking your personal nutrition to a thriving digital pet.
Our core value proposition is Accountability through Play. We simplify complex nutrition data into a visual journey that keeps 
users coming back every day.

Key Features:
AI Food Scanner: Take a photo of your meal or upload one from your gallery. The Gemini AI  identifies the food and provides an estimate of calories, protein, carbs, and fats.
AI Nutritional Search: A smart search bar that allows users to type in a food item and receive immediate nutritional breakdowns without manual entry.
Interactive "NutriCat" Dashboard: Features a visual ring progress tracker for daily calories and a pixel-art cat avatar that "evolves" (changes stages at 0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%) based on how close you are to your daily goal.
Personalized Onboarding: A step-by-step setup that calculates a custom calorie target based on biological sex, age, height, weight, activity level, and specific weight goals.
Goal Management: A dedicated tab for setting and tracking long-term weight targets and deadlines, providing a high-level view of your progress.
Categorized Meal Logging: Organized logging for Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks, with support for manual entries if needed.

Developed under the theme "Do What Makes You Happy", PETFIT focuses on:
Well-being: Making the often-tedious task of calorie tracking a rewarding and engaging habit.
Utility: Driving long-term consistency through gamified accountability, ensuring users stay engaged with their health goals.

Technologies Used:
Frontend: React Native / Expo, TypeScript 
Backend: Node.js, AsyncStorage 
AI & Tools: Gemini API, GitHub
